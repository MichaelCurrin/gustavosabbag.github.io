Summarized Curriculum

I am a PhD student in Political Science at the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG) with CAPES scholarship, MSc in Political Science and graduated in Social Sciences by Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar). Researcher in the project "Political Representation and Quality of Democracy: A Study of Latin America's Parliamentary Elites", from the Center for Legislative Studies (CEL), at UFMG, and from the "Latin American Political Parties Nucleus of Studies" (NEPPLA), from UFSCar. I have experience in political parties, local government, democratic accountability and electoral mobilization. My official CV can be found here.
